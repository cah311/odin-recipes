Burger Sauce

The Ultimate Burger Sauce

Get ready to take your burger game to the next level. This burger sauce gives you that nostalgic "special sauce" experience, while providing an unexpected kick.

Ingredients
  • 6 parts Mayo
  • 1 part Ketchup
  • 1/8 part cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 part dill pickle juice
  • Finely chopped dill pickle
  • Finely chopped California Chilli Pepeprs
Steps
  1. Mix all sauce and spice ingredients to the desired amount/li>
  2. Add chopped pickles and chilli peppers to a consistency where they are evenly distrubted throughout the sauce, but the sauce still remains a "sauce" consistency. (not just a chunky paste)
  3. Store in air tight jar or container and put in the fridge
