Fries

Fries that will melt your eyes

These fries go as the perfect complement to a burger, sandwhich, or stand alone as an amazing meal by themself. Use these in a poutine to blow your mind

Ingredients
  • 1 - 2 russet potatoes per person
Steps
  1. Cut potatoes into slices of about 1.5 cm in width
  2. Cut slices into uniform fries with the same width as before
  3. Soak fries in salt water for at least an hour, or over night for best results
  4. Pat dry before frying
  5. Heat up deep fryer to lowest setting (or around 300)
  6. Cook fries until slightly flimsy and colored
  7. Remove fries and heat oil to 425 or highest setting
  8. Finish fries until golden brown and crispy
  9. Season with sea salt
